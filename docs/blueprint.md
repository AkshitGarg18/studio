# **App Name**: Streak Keeper

## Core Features:

- Daily Progress Input: Allows students to input their daily progress and learning activities.
- Streak Tracking: Tracks the student's consecutive days of progress.
- Personalized Streak Goal: AI-powered tool assesses the user's typical progress, and proposes a personalized streak goal that challenges the student appropriately.
- Streak Visualization: Provides visual representation of the student's current and past streaks.
- Streak Loss Notification: Sends a notification to the student if they are at risk of losing their streak, encouraging them to maintain consistency. Generative AI analyzes user's prior activity and responses to notifications, to choose the optimal time, method, and phrasing of reminder message.
- Progress Storage: Saves daily progress and streak information.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep sky blue (#3498db) to reflect consistency and progress.
- Background color: Light gray (#ecf0f1) for a clean and focused interface.
- Accent color: Emerald green (#2ecc71) to highlight successful streaks and positive progress.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif font that provides a modern and neutral look suitable for both headlines and body text.
- Use clear and consistent icons to represent progress, goals, and notifications.
- A clean and intuitive layout, focusing on the streak and daily progress input.
- Subtle animations to celebrate milestones and streak achievements.
